The Integrity Project currently has franchise opportunities available in the United States. Current franchise areas are shaded in the map above for the state of Georgia.

If you have a heart for people and you believe in their ability to grow and improve themselves and their lives, you might be a candidate for a franchise.

The initial franchise fee is as low as $5000.00. Franchise investments typically begin at about $10,000.00 and go up from there. Some of the factors that affect franchise investments are: rent, target-area population, marketing, equipment purchases, office supplies, and training expenses.

In addition to the initial franchise fee, the franchisee must pay royalty fees to The Integrity Project(R) and meet certain other requirements.
